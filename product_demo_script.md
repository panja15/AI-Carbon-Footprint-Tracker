# EcoAI Product Demonstration Script (2-Minute Cut)

**Target Audience:** Hackathon Judges, Product Reviewers, and Technical Evaluators  
**Goal:** Showcase EcoAI’s unique value, user journey, and AI capabilities under 2 minutes.

---

## Timing & Flow Summary

| Section | Duration | Cumulative Time | Focus Page |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Opening** | 12s | 0:00 - 0:12 | `/login` → `/dashboard` (loading) |
| **2. User Onboarding** | 20s | 0:12 - 0:32 | `/audit` (Conversational Chat Audit) |
| **3. Sustainability Audit** | 18s | 0:32 - 0:50 | `/audit` (Carbon Breakdown Cards) |
| **4. Carbon Twin** | 25s | 0:50 - 1:15 | `/carbon-twin` (Current vs. Future comparison) |

---

## Script & Screen Directions

### 1. Opening (0:00 - 0:12)
* **Screen Action:**
  - Start on the `/login` page showing the premium dark UI.
  - Enter credentials quickly and click **Log In**. 
  - Show the `/dashboard` briefly loading.
* **Narration:**
  > "Most carbon tracking apps require users to manually enter data and only tell them what already happened. 
  > EcoAI acts as a active sustainability coach that helps users understand their footprint, explore better choices, and plan a lower-impact future."

---

### 2. User Onboarding (0:12 - 0:32)
* **Screen Action:**
  - Click on **Audit** in the left navigation sidebar.
  - Select **Option A: Conversational Audit**.
  - Type or paste the following text: 
    *`"I drive 15 km daily, eat a mixed diet, live in a household of four, and use AI tools frequently."`*
  - Hit Send. Show the AI loader spinning as it extracts the structured data.
  - Reveal the generated **Sustainability Persona** ("High AI Consumer / Daily Commuter") and the starting **Carbon Footprint Summary**.
* **Narration:**
  > "Instead of filling long, tedious forms, users can simply describe their lifestyle in plain language. EcoAI instantly parses their input and builds a structured, personalized sustainability profile."

---

### 3. Sustainability Audit (0:32 - 0:50)
* **Screen Action:**
  - Transition to the Audit breakdown charts/cards.
  - Hover over the **Transport** and **Electricity** contribution cards.
  - Highlight the top emission source card showing the primary emission category.
* **Narration:**
  > "EcoAI performs a complete sustainability audit, automatically mapping the user's details to localized India-specific emission factors—and immediately flags their largest source of pollution."

---

### 4. Carbon Twin (0:50 - 1:15)
* **Screen Action:**
  - Click **Carbon Twin** in the sidebar.
  - Show the side-by-side comparison cards: **Current You** (e.g. *1.8 tons CO2/year*) and **Future You** (e.g. *1.1 tons CO2/year*).
  - Point to the **38% Carbon Reduction** badge.
  - Scroll down to show the **Future Lifestyle Projections** narrative generated by the AI Coach.
* **Narration:**
  > "Next, EcoAI creates a Carbon Twin—a digital double of the user. By matching their reduction goals with localized grid averages, it projects a realistic, lower-impact future and displays a custom-tailored narrative outlining exactly how to reach it."

---

### 5. Decision Coach (1:15 - 1:35)
* **Screen Action:**
  - Click **Decision Coach** in the sidebar.
  - In the prompt field, type: *`"Should I drive or take the metro tomorrow?"`*
  - Click **Compare**.
  - Display the comparison output showing travel times, carbon offsets, and the clear recommendation to take the Delhi Metro.
* **Narration:**
  > "Instead of simply reporting emissions after the fact, EcoAI serves as an active decision tool. Users can query real-world scenarios to get instant, mathematically backed carbon trade-off comparisons."

---

### 6. Receipt Scanner (1:35 - 1:55)
* **Screen Action:**
  - Click **Receipt Scanner** in the sidebar.
  - Click **Upload Receipt** and select the [receipt.png](file:///c:/Users/tusharpanja/avenger%204.0/my%20learnings/AI-Carbon-Footprint-Tracker/receipt.png) image.
  - Show the OCR extraction progress bar.
  - Display the OCR review form populated with item names and estimated carbon footprint calculations.
* **Narration:**
  > "To automate logging, users can upload receipt images. Using Google Cloud Vision OCR, EcoAI extracts item names and costs, categorizes them, and calculates emissions deterministically on the spot."

---

### 7. Dashboard Summary & Closing (1:55 - 2:20)
* **Screen Action:**
  - Return to the **Dashboard** page.
  - Hover over the **Streak Tracker** in the header.
  - Expand the **AI Usage Awareness** widget to show the water and electrical footprint of using the LLM.
  - Finish on the full dashboard view.
* **Narration:**
  > "Everything consolidates back into our central dashboard. Users track their logging streak, monitor their eco-grade, and even see the environmental footprint of using AI itself.
  > EcoAI goes beyond carbon tracking. It combines AI guidance, active decision support, future planning, and automated data capture into a single platform designed to help users make better environmental choices every single day."

---

## 💡 Live Presentation Tips for Presenter
- **Pre-fill fields**: Keep prompt templates and test inputs copied to your clipboard to paste them instantly and save typing time.
- **Pre-upload receipt**: Have the test receipt image ready on your desktop for quick dragging-and-dropping.
- **Maintain a steady pace**: The transition from `/carbon-twin` to `/decision-coach` should be quick—let the screens do the talking while you focus on delivering a high-energy voiceover.
